Room additions – Everyone can use a space or two, and if you feel the need to have more space, Fidelity Builders can come up with a plan to accommodate your need for an additional space.

Insulation – Cali weather can be unpredictable at times and comfortability can be an issue. Therefore, it’s best to stay cool during hot summer days and cozy during cold winter season! Our insulation will solve all your predicaments when your temperature level is in jeopardy.

Engineering & Architectural services – In addition to home remodeling needs, we also provide engineering and architectural services to help expedite any tests and permits required by the city.

Exterior Paint – When you want to give your exterior a brand-new look. We can help pick out the color that will match the existing theme of your house and do the labor work for you as well.

Roofing – We can inspect the entire condition of your roof and offer different services on how to improve the appearance and quality of it.

Bathroom Remodeling – We can custom design the look and feel of your bathroom by adding fixtures that complement your whole bathroom theme.

HVAC – We can install HVAC to provide heating and cooling systems that will allow for a more comfortable environment for you and your family.

Windows and Doors – We can install brand new doors and windows and help you choose which designs are more appropriate to match the existing house.

Flooring – Floor designs are just as important. If you need to brighten up the house, a new floor installation might be needed. Depending on the theme of the house, there are different customized floorings to choose from - carpet, vinyl, stone, woods and many more.
